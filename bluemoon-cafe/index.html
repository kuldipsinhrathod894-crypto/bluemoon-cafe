Our Story

A cafe born under the open sky

Tucked away on Bypass Road in Himatnagar, Blue Moon Cafe is where locals come to unwind. Fairy lights, colorful seating, and a relaxed outdoor vibe — we've made evenings something to look forward to.

From morning chai to late-night mocktails, we're open whenever you need us — even till 2 in the morning.

Beautiful outdoor seating with fairy light ambience
Dine-in, takeaway & online ordering available
Pet-friendly outdoor space
Perfect for dates, hangouts & family evenings
